next-lang-decimal

description:

  • A decimal lib based on next toolkit.

usage:

  • Get Decimal util:
  var Decimal=nx.lang.Decimal;
  • Add:
  Decimal.add(0.1,0.2,0.3);
  • Sub:
  Decimal.sub(0.3,0.1);
  • mul:
  Decimal.mul(0.3,0.1,0.2);
  • div:
  Decimal.mul(0.3,0.1);

test:

  • cd next-lang-decimal
  • mocha
